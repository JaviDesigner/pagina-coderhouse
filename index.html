the English language and me

in school, they only taught me the verb 'to be,' and it wasn't much different in college. I didn't really meet many people who spoke English, except for the English teachers (haha). This topic didn't have much importance in my life before, but now it does, and it's hitting me hard..

Why am I doing this?

now I live abroad, and you wouldn't believe how hard it is not to understand a thing. It's frustrating and closes so many doors professionally and socially. I'm trying to share my story so someone else might do something before reaching this point and also to let off some steam.
